Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this personal website proposal is to create a high school football website that serves as a digital football program, providing information on the team's roster, schedule, and explanations of the game's rules. By functioning as a digital football program, the website aims to offer an easily accessible and comprehensive resource for these stakeholders, enhancing the overall experience for parents and fans of the team.

Audience

The intended audience for this website is parents and fans of the high school football team, offering them easy access to relevant information about the team and the sport. The goal of this website aims to strengthen the connection between the high school football team, its fans, and their families by providing a user-friendly and informative online platform. It will accomplish this by: 1. Providing a detailed team roster, including player names, numbers, positions, and grade levels, ensuring that supporters can stay informed about the individuals on the team. 2. Present a well-organized schedule, displaying information about upcoming games, opponents, game times, and locations, making it convenient for fans to plan their attendance. 3. Feature a dedicated rules page, designed to provide valuable insights and explanations about the official calls made during football games. This not only educates parents about the intricacies of the sport but also ensures a deeper engagement with the game.

Welcome

Welcome to the exhilarating play of Trivium Prep Football! We invite students, parents, and devoted fans to join us in celebrating the passion, dedication, and unwavering spirit of our remarkable team. Trivium's football program is not just about touchdowns and victories; it's a vibrant community that fosters character development, teamwork, and a love for the game. Whether you're a student athlete striving for greatness, a supportive parent cheering from the sidelines, or an enthusiastic fan seeking thrilling Friday night lights, we are excited to have you as a part of our football family. Together, we'll embark on a journey of grit and glory, creating memories that will last a lifetime. Get ready to experience the electrifying energy of Trivium Prep Football!

Great Hearts Athletic Philosophy

Our students benefit from the development of habits and virtue as part of the athletic experience. We strive for excellence on and off the field, providing an experience that shapes our athletes to be better people. The best measure of success of a Great Hearts athletic program is found in the character of the athlete it produces. All of our students can find opportunities to be successful while also being challenged at the highest levels of athletic competition.

Great Hearts athletes train and practice with vigor similar to their approach in their academic pursuits. Our athletic programs foster a sense of cooperation that builds community and creates the life-long friendships. Our programs also foster team spirit and encourage the celebration of accomplishments and personal growth. Through community and building relationships, athletes are held to the highest standard of integrity and sportsmanship.

Good Sportsmanship Reminder

As member school of the Arizona Interscholastic Association, we would like to remind you the objectives of educational based athletics are what set this event apart from all other levels of competition. We strive to promote good sportsmanship by student-athletes, coaches and spectators. We have a strong commitment to excellence, fair play, and above all, good sportsmanship. We request your cooperation by supporting the participants and officials in a positive manner.
